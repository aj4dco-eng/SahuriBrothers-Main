# Sahuri Bros - Brand Guide & Strategic Positioning

## Company Overview
**Sahuri Bros.** (אחיוות סחורי) is a luxury climate engineering and HVAC solutions company specializing in invisible, integrated climate control systems for high-end residential and commercial projects.

## Mission Statement
"We engineer invisible climate solutions that disappear into architecture and emerge as perfect comfort."

---

## Brand Architecture

### Three Core Pillars

#### 1. **Engineering Precision (דיוק הנדסי)**
- **Concept**: We don't hide air conditioning units, we design spaces without them.
- **Key Messages**:
  - "We solve architectural challenges other companies can't."
  - "Floor-level micro grilles, hidden ceiling ducts, integrated wall systems."
  - "VRF systems designed with CAD precision to meet zero-visibility standards."
- **Proof Points**: Case studies showing complex installations (glass ceilings, hidden units, sub-floor heating).

#### 2. **The Bros. Guarantee (ערבות משפחתית)**
- **Concept**: When you work with a corporate institution, you get a project manager. When you work with Sahuri Bros., you get the brothers themselves.
- **Key Messages**:
  - "Our name is on every project."
  - "Artisans who obsess over details—from installation to after-sales service."
  - "Personal accountability from start to finish."
- **Proof Points**: Team bios, installation videos, customer testimonials emphasizing personal service.

#### 3. **Luxury Comfort (נוחות פאר)**
- **Concept**: Perfect climate, silently. A five-star hotel experience in your home.
- **Key Messages**:
  - "We don't sell machines, we deliver the perfect climate."
  - "Total silence, perfect temperature, always."
  - "Climate control as an invisible part of luxury living."
- **Proof Points**: Lifestyle imagery, customer experiences of refined comfort.

## Brand Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Blue `#1a5490` (Precision, Trust, Professionalism)
- **Secondary**: Metallic Gray `#666666` (Technology, Refinement)
- **Accent**: Bronze/Gold `#B8860B` (Luxury, Warmth)
- **Neutral**: Pure White `#FFFFFF`, Dark Charcoal `#1a1a1a`

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Modern sans-serif, geometric, clean (suggest: Helvetica Neue, Montserrat)
- **Body**: Readable sans-serif, professional (suggest: Open Sans, Roboto)
- **Tagline Font**: Thin, elegant, minimal

### Logo
- **Format**: Horizontal wordmark with optional tagline
- **Style**: Geometric, minimalist; reflects engineering precision
- **Variations**:
  - `SahuriLogotype.ai` - Full logo with "מהנדסי אקלים" subtitle
  - `SahuriIconMark.ai` - Symbol only (geometric representation of climate control—air flow, temperature, precision)

### Imagery Style
- **Primary**: High-end luxury residential and commercial spaces
- **Tone**: Clean, minimalist, architectural focus
- **Color Grading**: Warm, natural light; cool/blue tones for technology
- **People**: Diverse, professional engineers and craftspeople at work; satisfied, wealthy clients in their homes

## Target Audiences & Messaging

### B2C: Luxury Homeowners (Private Residents)
- **Demographics**: HNI/UHNWI, Jerusalem & Tel Aviv metro
- **Pain Points**: "I want perfect climate but don't want to see the equipment."
- **Messaging Focus**: Luxury Comfort + Engineering Precision
- **Channels**: Instagram; Facebook; LinkedIn (referrals); Direct (word-of-mouth)

### B2A: Architects & Designers
- **Demographics**: Registered architects, high-end interior designers, design firms
- **Pain Points**: "My design vision is compromised by mechanical requirements."
- **Messaging Focus**: Engineering Precision + The Bros. Guarantee
- **Channels**: LinkedIn; Architecture publications; Design collaborations
- **Key Selling Point**: "The secret weapon architects trust"

### B2B: Developers & Contractors
- **Demographics**: Luxury residential developers, premium construction companies
- **Pain Points**: "HVAC is a liability in my high-end projects; I need partners, not suppliers."
- **Messaging Focus**: Luxury Comfort + The Bros. Guarantee
- **Channels**: LinkedIn; Industry events; Direct B2B partnerships
- **Key Selling Point**: "Value-add through design integration"

## Visual Systems

### Photography Guidelines
- **Architecture**: Clean lines, natural light, focus on the absence of visible HVAC
- **People**: Engineers and craftspeople in professional settings; satisfied clients in their homes
- **Technology**: CAD renderings, thermal imaging, precision-focused detail shots

### Graphic Elements
- Geometric shapes reflecting climate flow (circles, flowing lines)
- Minimal iconography (temperature icon, precision marks, air flow symbols)
- Subtle metallic/gradient accents to suggest technology + refinement

---

## Non-Negotiable Brand Standards

1. **Never** use cheap imagery or before/after comparisons
2. **Never** use loud colors (primary blue is the only pop of color)
3. **Never** make heavy use of technical jargon in marketing (translate to benefits)
4. **Never** position against competitors (focus on value, not comparison)
5. **Always** emphasize the human touch ("the brothers")
6. **Always** lead with the problem solved, not the product sold
